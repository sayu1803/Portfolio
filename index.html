MedTrack

MedTrack is a comprehensive web application designed to streamline the management of personal medical histories, providing users with a secure, intuitive platform for storing and accessing their health information. This application features robust user authentication and data encryption to ensure privacy and security. Users can log their medical records, track their health metrics, and upload essential documents such as lab results and prescriptions. MedTrack also includes an interactive chatbot interface to assist with common medical queries and offers personalized health tips based on the user's medical history. With informative medical pages, users can stay informed about various health conditions and treatments. The user-friendly design and responsive interface make it easy for individuals to manage their health data from any device, promoting better health management and informed decision-making.
